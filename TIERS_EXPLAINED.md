## The Three Options Users See

### Option 1: Free Trial (1,000 Credits)
**Cost:** $0
**Requirement:** Email only
**Duration:** One-time, until 1,000 credits exhausted
**What They Get:** Full access to all modules, courses, features for 1,000 credits worth

### Option 2: GrowthPlus Membership ($9.99/month)
**Cost:** $9.99/month (recurring)
**Billing:** Monthly, charged on same date
**Cancellation:** Anytime, no penalty
**Duration:** Monthly subscription

**What They Get:**
- 10,000 additional credits PER MONTH
- Full access to all modules, courses, features
- Founding member price locked in (never increases)

**Example:**
- Month 1: 1,000 free + 10,000 GrowthPlus = 11,000 total credits
- Month 2: 10,000 GrowthPlus credits (new month)
- Month 3: 10,000 GrowthPlus credits (new month)
- If they cancel Month 4: No charges after cancellation

**Best For:** Users who want convenience + don't want to manage API keys

---

### Option 3: Your Own Free API Key (Groq or OpenRouter)
**Cost:** $0 (forever)
**Requirement:** 2-minute setup
**Duration:** Unlimited, as long as free tier available

**What They Get:**
- Completely free access to Groq or OpenRouter
- Unlimited credits (no limit from us)
- Full control over their own account

**How It Works:**
1. User goes to console.groq.com or openrouter.ai
2. Creates free account (2 min)
3. Gets their own API key
4. Adds it to GrowthHub Settings
5. Now has unlimited access (costs them $0)

**Best For:** Technical users, power users, users who want maximum value

## What "Unlimited Free" Means

**NOT:** Unlimited use of GrowthHub's free tier

**YES:** Unlimited use with their own free API key
- Groq free tier = unlimited (forever, no payment)
- OpenRouter free tier = unlimited (forever, no payment)
- They control their own usage
